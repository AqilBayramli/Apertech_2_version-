About Us

Find Out More About Us

Apertech Solutions is a technology and business consulting company based in Baku, Azerbaijan, dedicated to delivering innovative and efficient Erp solutions to organizations of all sizes. Since its establishment, Apertech has focused on helping companies streamline their business processes, improve decision-making, and achieve digital transformation through reliable, scalable, and customized software systems.

We specialize in enterprise resource planning (ERP), financial management, business intelligence, and custom software development. As an official regional partner of Infor SunSystems, we provide world-class financial and accounting solutions trusted by global enterprises. Additionally, our own in-house product, Genesis, offers advanced tools for procurement management, budgeting, contract tracking, and data analytics — helping businesses maintain full control over their operations.

At Apertech, we believe that every organization is unique, and so are its technology needs. That’s why we take a personalized approach — from initial consulting and system design to integration, implementation, and long-term support. Our experienced specialists ensure that each solution is tailored to the client’s goals, industry, and workflow.
